The Weekend

The festivities all take place in and around Letchworth State Park in New York. Here are the details as they currently stand, but check back for updates!

BBQ Barn Party

Everyone is invited to the welcome dinner and barn party, which will happen at Maple Walnut Farms on July 15.

Ceremony and Reception

The ceremony will take place at the historic Glen Iris Inn at 4pm on July 16 with drinks, dinner, and dancing immediately to follow in a tent next door.

Farewell Brunch

Before you head home, have a bite to eat and a cup of coffee on Sunday morning.
