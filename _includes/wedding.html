The Weekend

Letchworth State Park, New York

BBQ Barn Party

Everyone is invited to the welcome dinner and barn party at Maple Walnut Farms on July 15. Feel free to come at 6; food by Dinosaur BBQ will be served around 7, with music to follow. Later arrivals also welcome!

Ceremony and Reception

The ceremony will take place at the historic Glen Iris Inn at 4pm on July 16 with drinks, dinner, and dancing immediately to follow. Buses to the wedding depart from both hotels at 2:45. A second bus leaves only Country Inn and Suites at 3pm.

Farewell Brunch

Both hotels offer free breakfast, but there will also be a light breakfast available at Caroline's restuarant in the Glen Iris Inn at 10am. If you don't have time to eat, you can still stop by to say farewell any time between 10 and 12.
